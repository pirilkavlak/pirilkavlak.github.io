About Me

I am a Growth & Product Marketing expert with a decade of experience scaling SaaS and developer-focused products. I focus on building sustainable growth engines, optimizing the entire user journey — from acquisition and activation to engagement and retention.

Currently at Catchpoint, I lead the GTM and growth of WebPageTest. I own its freemium and self‑serve motion, SEO, major product launches, and the integration into Catchpoint's Website Experience solution, partnering closely with product and cross‑functional teams to accelerate adoption and retention across enterprise and self‑serve users.

Beyond corporate roles, I've supported early‑stage startups in diagnosing growth bottlenecks and building their go‑to‑market engines.

I live at the intersection of product and marketing, where growth is a discipline to be built, tested, and scaled.
